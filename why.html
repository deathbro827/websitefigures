Why

Many people now knows what anime is, so it's not uncommon for people to know what it is. You can easily get one online or from your local store.

For myself, it's my hobby where it's kinda like a habit to collect things, so getting an anime figure is pretty hard to resist. I have collected mostly from second hand or off brand as first rated figures may sometime be really expensive. (and saves me money at least!)

Getting the off brand instead of first rated is hardly any differences. Although, you still have to take into consideration if the materials being made are durable or if their appearence are made properly. If needed, you can get the first rated figures if you really like it.

All in all, happy shopping!
